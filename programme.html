Attila Berczes

University of Debreceni, Hungary

Binary recurrence sequences have been extensively researched in the context of Diophantine number theory. In this talk, Attila Berczes will explore results on Diophantine equations and problems containing linear recurrence sequences, where these sequences play a crucial role in the proofs.

Lajos Hajdu

University of Debrecen, Hungary

This presentation delves into the properties of Catalan numbers, proving that the product of two different Catalan numbers cannot be a perfect square. Additional results include characterizations of powerful numbers and related Diophantine problems.
